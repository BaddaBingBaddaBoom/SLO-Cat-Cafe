SLO Cat Cafe is dedicated to providing a calm and relaxing setting for anyone looking to destress.

Experience a cafe, but with cats.

Cat cafes originated from Taiwan, but the idea was popularized in Japan. You won't see many here in the Western hemisphere, so don't miss out on this chance! Simply buy a drink, play with cats, and relax.
